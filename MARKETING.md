# Knots: Your Personal Chief of Staff

**Stop juggling tabs. Start shipping what matters.**

---

## The Problem

You have action items scattered across Monday.com, Slack, email, and meeting notes. You copy-paste between tools, lose track of commitments, and walk into 1-on-1s wondering "what did I promise last week?" Your to-do list doesn't know about your goals, and your goals don't connect to your tasks.

## Meet Knots

Knots is a lightweight personal operating system that pulls your work into one place and keeps it aligned with what actually matters.

**Try it now:** https://v0-knots-taker.vercel.app/

---

## What You Get

### One Inbox for Everything
Action items from Monday.com sync automatically every 30 minutes. No more switching between tools to figure out what's on your plate. Mark items Done or Dismiss them, and the status syncs back to Monday.

### Tasks That Move With You
Drag-and-drop your priorities. Snooze what's not urgent (tomorrow, 3 days, next week) and it disappears until you need it. Completed tasks slide away with a satisfying animation and land in your Backlog as a record of what you shipped.

### Weekly Goals That Connect to Work
Set P0/P1/P2 goals for the week. Link tasks to goals so you can see at a glance whether your daily work aligns with your priorities. Goals auto-flag as "at risk" when deadlines approach. Paste a 1-on-1 transcript and let AI extract your goals for you.

### People Context at Your Fingertips
Before your next call, pull up a person's profile: their role, strengths, growth areas, communication style, current focus, and timezone. No more scrambling for context.

### A Backlog That Works
Snoozed items, completed tasks, and parked ideas all live in one organized backlog. Items return automatically when their snooze expires. Nothing falls through the cracks.

- **Real-time sync.** Open it on your phone and laptop. Changes appear instantly on both.

## 2-Minute Setup

1. Sign in with your email
2. Enter your name and role
3. Connect your Monday.com board (just paste your Board ID)
4. Start working

That's it. Your Monday action items will appear within 30 minutes, or hit the sync button to pull them immediately.
